Dave Lemke is a former bassist and backing vocalist for Imagine Dragons, serving as one of the band's founding members from 2008 to 2009. A graduate of Brigham Young University, he joined the original lineup alongside Dan Reynolds, Andrew Tolman, Andrew Beck, and Aurora Florence, performing on the group's debut project, the Speak to Me EP. Although he remained with the band longer than Beck and Florence, he eventually departed in 2009 before the release of their self-titled EP, leading to his replacement by Ben McKee. Outside of his early musical career, Lemke has established himself as a professional in the financial and technology sectors, serving as a tax partner at PwC and a former executive at the digital asset platform Giddy, while also contributing his expertise to the Utah Blockchain and Digital Innovation Task Force.
