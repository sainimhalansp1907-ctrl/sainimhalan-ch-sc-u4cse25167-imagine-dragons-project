Wayne Sermon (born June 15, 1984, in American Fork, Utah, USA) is an American musician best known as the lead guitarist and backing vocalist of the Grammy-winning rock band Imagine Dragons. He studied guitar and music theory at Berklee College of Music, which strongly influenced his clean, melodic, and atmospheric playing style that blends rock, pop, and electronic elements. Wayne is also involved in songwriting and production, contributing significantly to the band's polished sound. Known for being private and low-key, he avoids media attention and focuses primarily on music rather than celebrity life. His height is commonly listed around 5'9" (175 cm), though it has never been officially confirmed.
