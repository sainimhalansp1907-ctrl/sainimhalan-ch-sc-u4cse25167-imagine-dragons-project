Andrew Beck is an American musician and illustrator who was a founding member of Imagine Dragons in 2008. While studying graphic design at Brigham Young University, he was recruited by lead singer Dan Reynolds to play guitar and keyboards, providing backing vocals for the band's initial lineup. Beck performed on the group's debut EP, Speak to Me, before departing in late 2008 as the band's musical direction began to shift. Following his exit, he built a prolific career as a freelance illustrator, with his work appearing in major publications such as GQ, The New York Times, and The Wall Street Journal. He has remained active in the music scene, later joining the psychedelic rock band Day Sounds and currently performing as a vocalist and guitarist for the baroque pop group The Mellons.
