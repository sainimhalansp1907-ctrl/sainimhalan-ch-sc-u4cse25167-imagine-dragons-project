Daniel Platzman (born September 28, 1986, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA) is an American musician best known as the former drummer of Imagine Dragons. He studied film scoring at Berklee College of Music, which influenced his rhythmic precision and cinematic approach to percussion. Beyond drumming, Daniel is a composer, producer, and filmmaker, often working on visual projects and short films. Known for his creativity and artistic range, he officially departed Imagine Dragons in August 2024 to focus on film and solo work.
