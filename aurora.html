Aurora Florence is a musician and actress who was a founding member of Imagine Dragons in 2008 while studying at Brigham Young University. During her time with the group, she contributed as a multi-instrumentalist—playing piano, violin, and providing additional vocals—and performed on the band’s debut EP, Speak to Me. After departing the lineup in early 2009, she moved to New York City to pursue a career in musical theater, famously starring as Eliza Doolittle in the national and international tours of My Fair Lady. As a solo artist, she released the EP It’s Wonderful in 2013 and has appeared in various stage productions, including the musical Once. Today, she continues to perform, write original music, and share her expertise as a vocal and instrumental coach through her own teaching studio.
