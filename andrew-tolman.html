Andrew Tolman is an American musician best known as the original drummer of Imagine Dragons during the band’s early and formative years from around 2008 to 2011. As part of the initial lineup, he played an important role in shaping the group’s early sound, contributing to their rhythm, live performances, and early recordings while they were still performing locally and building a following. Tolman left the band before their major commercial breakthrough, which later came with chart-topping songs such as “It’s Time” and “Radioactive.” After his departure, he continued to pursue music on his own and explore other creative paths, remaining largely outside the mainstream spotlight as Imagine Dragons rose to global fame.
